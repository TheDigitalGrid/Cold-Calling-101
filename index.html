1. The Essentials of Cold Calling

Cold calling is about making connections, not just pitching. The key components of a successful cold call include:

  • Clearly stating your purpose and value proposition.
  • Engaging in a two-way conversation, not a monologue.
  • Handling objections with confidence.
  • Ending with a clear next step.
